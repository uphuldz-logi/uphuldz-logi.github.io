The procedure to set up an Uphold profile via mobile or PC

You can easily get a new Uphold profile using your mobile or PC with the help of the steps that are stated below:

  1. Open Uphold mobile app on your mobile

Note: If you have not installed the app yet, go to the App or Play Store on your device to finish the app installation process.

  1. Or on your PC, launch a web browser and go to the Uphold.com webpage

  3. On this page, type the name, email, password, and other details to proceed
  4. When asked, you need to agree to the Uphold terms of use on the same page
  5. If you have, provide the referral code details in the corresponding fields
  6. Now, on the next page, complete the email verification process
  7. Finally, you have completed the Uphold login account setup process

Method to access the Uphold login account on PC

You can easily access your Uphold account using a PC or mobile. If you are not sure how to access Uphold on a PC, you need to follow the steps that are given below:

  1. On your PC, open a preferred browser
  2. Go to the Uphold.com/login page using this browser
  3. Now, you need to type the username and password details
  4. Finally, you will be logged in to your Uphold account using the PC

The quickest way to recover the Uphold account

You may require to recover your Uphold account in case you have lost the password details. In the situation of too many failed logins, you can follow the steps that are given below:

  1. Open a browser or launch the Uphold mobile app
  2. Here, discover the ‘Forgot Password’ button and click on it
  3. Now, you will be taken to the Uphold login account recovery page
  4. On this page, type the email address to get the reset link
  5. Now, you need to find the verification link and click on it
  6. After that, you need to set a new password in the respective field
  7. Once you type the password details, verify them carefully
  8. Now, save the new password details to complete this process
